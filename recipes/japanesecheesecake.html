Japanese Cheesecake

Light, jiggly, and fluffy, Japanese Cheesecake (Souffle Cheesecake) is seriously the most delicious treat to serve for a crow. It will melt in your mouth. A second (or more) serving is guaranteed

Ingredients

  • 60g whole milk
  • 140g cream cheese
  • 40g butter (room temperature)
  • 40g all-purpose flour
  • 15g corn starch (2 Tbsp)
  • 5 eggs (white and yolk separated)
  • 75g sugar

Steps

  1. Fill cooking tray with room temperature water until 1cm high. Preheat tray in overn to 250ºF.
  2. Mix whole milk, cream cheese, butter in pot over low heat. Mix with whisk until fully melted. Do not let it boil. Take it off heat
  3. Whisk in all-purpose flour and corn starch. Keep stiiring until mixture is thick and fully combined.
  4. Add 5 egg yolk into mixture.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk with an electric beater 5 egg whites until soft-medium peaks. Add 75g fine sugar while white and runny.
  6. Fold in egg white to egg yolk mixture.
  7. Pour into greased pan. Use toothpick to make switls. Tap on counter to remove bubbles.
  8. Bake at 250ºF for 20 minutes. Then, 320ºF for 20 minutes and 220ºF for 30 minutes.
