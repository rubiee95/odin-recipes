Ramen Egg

Known as Ajitsuke Tamago or Ajitama in Japanese, Ramen Eggs are delicious as a topping on ramen or enjoyed as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin

Ingredients

  1. Boil pot of water. Add eggs once boiled. Cook for 8 minutes.
  2. Put cooked eggs into cold bath for at least 3 minutes.
  3. Peal shell and add into soy sauce and mirin mixture.
  4. Refrigerate for >4 hours or overnight.
